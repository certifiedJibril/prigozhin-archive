The Prigozhin Archive

A bilingual corpus of interviews, statements, and documents from Evgeny Prigozhin.

About the Project

This digital archive collects, preserves, and offers full-text search of over a thousand interviews and documents related to Evgeny Prigozhin. Each entry contains the original Russian version and its English translation, along with any attached images or PDFs.

Users can filter interviews by date, keywords in either language, and other metadata. This project was created to assist researchers, historians, and the general public in understanding the persona and public narrative of one of the most controversial figures in contemporary Russian history.
